About Our Shop

“E-Medhouse” is an E-commerce that solely exists with an aim to provide business-to-customer and business-to-business transactions of medicine, medical machinery, and equipment so that it is accessible when needed.

“E-Medhouse” also provides the users with the ability to book doctor and lab appointments online as well as a neat SEO-friendly user interface to ensure accessibility even for differently-abled people.

It intends to provide an amicable platform for the digitization of the health facilities and connect the related business groups too in order to provide the necessary services to the end-user.
